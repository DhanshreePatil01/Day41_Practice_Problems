Welcome To Famous Places Of India

Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is considered to be the greatest architectural achievement in the whole range of Indo-Islamic architecture.The Taj-Mahal is built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in 1653

Golden Temple

The Golden temple is famous for its full golden dome, it is one of the most sacred pilgrim spots for Sikhs.Golden Temple is located at Amritsar in Punjab. It was built in 1604 by the by Guru Arjan Dev.

Thar Desert in Rajasthan

The Great Indian Desert, Thar Desert is most famous camel safari destination, which is located in the north western of Rajashtan.

Kashmir-The Heaven on Earth

Kashmir is the most attractive and famous hill station destination.It is also called the Heaven due to its greenery beauty and fantastic snow-covered hills.

Jal Mahal

The beautiful Mughal-Rajput style palace Jal Mahal is located in the center of Mansagar lake. Jal Mahal is also known as “Water Palace”, Which is most sightseeing place in Jaipur, Rajasthan.

Manali- Stream of Beas River

The Beas River is the most attractive and beautiful sights in the Manali valley.The beautiful view and rippling sound of the river fascinate the lots of tourists.You may feel that you are in heaven.
